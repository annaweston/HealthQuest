  • CHA2DS2-VASc Score for Atrial Fibrillation Stroke Risk
    Your score is:
    Find out more about this test.

    This score has been developed in the light of studies that have investigated factors associated with risk of stroke in patients with AF. AF patients suffer strokes because they develop blood clots in the heart (left atrium) which break off and go through the circulation to the brain. Advancing age, having impaired heart function, high blood pressure, blood vessel (vascular) disease or diabetes and being Female (birth gender) are risk factors for this. Studies have shown that this score system enables stroke prevention (anticoagulation) treatments to be targeted to individuals so that the small risk of the treatment itself is greatly outweighed by the (measured) greater risk of a blood clot.

    If you are uncertain ask your doctor which of these may be relevant to you.
